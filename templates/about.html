🎨 Lighting Studio

3D Reference Tool for Artists

This interactive 3D tool helps artists study lighting, shadows, and form without needing complex 3D software. Perfect for reference when drawing or painting!

Features

💡 Dynamic Lighting: Place multiple lights to create custom lighting setups
📐 3D Objects: Add spheres and cubes to study how light affects different forms
🎮 Interactive Controls: Select, drag, scale, and position objects in 3D space
📏 Vertical Movement: Move objects up and down for complex compositions
🗑️ Easy Management: Delete and modify objects as needed
📱 Responsive: Works on desktop and tablet devices

How to Use

  1. Place objects: Click Light/Sphere/Cube buttons, then click on the ground
  2. Select objects: Use Select mode (V) to choose objects
  3. Move objects: Use Drag mode (D) to reposition selected objects
  4. Scale objects: Use Scale mode (R) and mouse wheel or slider
  5. Vertical movement: Hold Shift + scroll or use arrow keys
  6. Camera: Click and drag to rotate view, scroll to zoom

Perfect For

  • 📚 Art students learning about lighting and form
  • 🎨 Digital artists needing lighting reference
  • ✏️ Traditional artists studying shadow patterns
  • 👩‍🎨 Anyone wanting to understand how light works
